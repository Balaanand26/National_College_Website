BBA Airlines & Airport Management

BBA Airlines & Airport Management

This curriculum is designed with a conceptual structure in line with the standards of the Indian civil aviation industry, offered by very few universities. This curriculum is highly focused on preparing students to enter the aviation sector. In the present and future, there is a bright scope in this aviation sector, which is tailored to meet the needs of the job market in the growing aviation industry. This BBA program is designed for those who are seeking jobs in the aviation sector, and it is a 3-year degree.

Accreditation

National Institute Of Management & Science in collaboration with Alagappa University (A State University Accredited with A+ Grade & Category – 1 Graded University by MHRD - UGC) offers Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) under graduate program in Airlines and Airport management.

Eligibility

Qualification Course
12th or Any Diploma BBA Airlines & Airport Management

* subject to passing the our Aptitude test followed by Personal Interview.

Career Opportunities

This particular BBA in Airlines and Airport Management in Madurai offers multiple career choices to secure opportunities under various designations in the industry. These include

Air Ticketing, Flight Operations, Customer Service, Ramp Service, Passenger Service, Cabin Service, Aviation Security, Customer Support, Cargo Handling, Customer Relationship Management, Passenger Reservations, Revenue Management, Airline Sales & Marketing and Flight/Data Analysis.

Advantages of joining the BBA Airlines & Airport Management in National College

The BBA Degree Awarded by Alagappa University Provides a BBA Airlines curriculum That includes Grooming, Global Communication, Soft Skills & Personality Development.

  • Free In-Flight Training
  • On-the-Job Training at International Airport
  • The course comes with a Dual Certification, i.e., a BBA - Airlines degree from Alagappa University and a Certificate of Accomplishment from National College for the Postgraduate Program in Airline Job roles from the Central Government of India Certification.
  • All our trainers are well-experienced professionals from the Airlines Industry.
  • On successful completion of the BBA in Airlines and Airport Management, students may choose to further specialize by pursuing an MBA degree in Airlines & Airport Management at National College.
