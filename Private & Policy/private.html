PRIVACY POLICY

This Privacy Policy outlines the nature of the data we collect, how it is used, and the measures we take to protect it.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to all users who access or interact with our website at www.nationalcollege.co.in. By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information we collect

We collect two types of information

Personal information

Personal data may be collected when you submit forms or communicate with us via the website. This may include:

Full Name

Email Address

Contact Number

City/Location

Educational Background (as applicable)

Non-Personal Information

We may automatically collect technical data such as:

IP address

Browser type and version

Device information

Purpose of Data Collection

The information we collect is used strictly for the following purposes:

To respond to your inquiries and service requests

To improve website functionality and user experience

To send important updates or notifications (only with prior consent)

For internal analytics and reporting

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Information may be shared under the following circumstances:

With internal departments for academic or administrative processing

With trusted service providers engaged to maintain our website, under confidentiality agreements

As required by law or legal processes

Data Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. To prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, we implement appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures, including:

Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption

Access control measures for data storage

Routine website security audits

Use of Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance functionality and collect usage analytics. Cookies help us understand user preferences and improve site performance. You may disable cookies via your browser settings; however, this may impact certain features of the website.

Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

Request access to the personal data we hold about you

Request corrections or updates to your personal information

Withdraw consent for us to use your information (where applicable)

Request deletion of your data from our records

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details provided below.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such external sites. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of those websites separately.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the Site signifies acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
