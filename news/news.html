Fill the application form and submit with self-attested copies. Do not send original certificates. Apply online or submit directly at campus.

Offline Application forms can be received directly/in person from our College Campus.

The candidates who have passed the qualifying 12th / UG examination will be selected on the basis of our Future Leaders Scholarship Entrance Exam marks obtained, Direct Interview & subject to all criteria of eligibility and the availability of seats.

Date of counselling or Direct interview /online platform will be intimated to the candidate through mail.

Government of India in consultation with RBI and Indian Banker's Association has framed a educational loan scheme to ensure that no deserving student in the country is deprived of higher education for want of finances. The scheme covers all type of courses including Professional courses in India and abroad. Eligible students can apply in respective banks. Our Student’s Relationship Manager will guide our students to obtain 100% Education Loan with Leading Banks.

The students who have been selected in our Future Leaders Scholarship Entrance Exam / Direct interview must report to the College on the stipulated date and time as informed from the office along with the following

Copies of mark lists and proof of identity, age, qualification etc.

Four copies of recent passport size photograph.

The students on reaching the College are required to meet our Student’s Relationship Manager with the duly filled-in application form for enrolment. Our Student’s Relationship Manager then directs the student to the Interview Board. The students who are selected by the board will be directed to the accounts department for payment of fees. The parent & student must read and understand the terms & conditions form and course details before joining and the same must be signed and submitted to the administrator along with admission application form.

Accommodation is available for boys and girls in separate hostels. The students may make a choice of a particular hostel in consultation with College authorities. However the hostel chosen must be there in the approved list of the college authorities. S Students are requested to pay the hostel fee directly at the hostel. Inmates must follow the rules and regulations of the hostel.

Genuine placement is provided with a view to assist the students with high academic attainments for getting placements in International Airlines & Airports, International Banks, Shipping & Logistics Companies and Top International Companies.

Wearing uniform is compulsory on all working days since it brings every student to the same platform. This inculcates a feeling of equality amongst the students. The industry also demands it.

Our National College Students are expected to behave well and maintain discipline on Campus. Any complaint about discipline will be viewed seriously and appropriate action will be taken.

National College Trainers are mostly Industry experts only and has a team of highly efficient, qualified, competent and committed doctorate scholars in all subjects.
