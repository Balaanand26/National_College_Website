Professional Bankers Program

This Banking course is designed to Suite graduates aspiring for career in banking. All students who have successfully completed their graduation with min 50% score in their final year graduation are eligible for this program. Graduates from all streams are welcome to join this program subject to passing the Aptitude test followed personal Interview.

Salient Features of the Courses

Industry Relevant Curriculam:

The Curriculum for this Banking program has been designed by banking industry experts & has been appreciated by leading banks in India, making outcome result oriented.

Experienced Faculties:

Our Trainers are senior banking professionals that share their exceptional knowledge, Real life experiences & Case studies help students grasp the knowledge required to adopt in banking Industry.

Master Classes:

As part of finstream curriculam,Students have the chance to interact with Banking & Finance industry experts through selective master classes.

Activities and Workshops :

Activities are undertaken through out the year to ensure all round development skills among the students, including presentation techniques, Language Skills, Interview Skills, Team Building these make them industry Ready.

Industry Certification :

Opportunity for Industry Specific Certifications like NCFM,IIBF-CAIIB,CFP & etc.

Certified Financial Planner - CFP

CFP Certification program makes you competent to provide strategic advice in investments, insurance, tax, retirement & real estate planning. It is an global certification provided by FPSB USA which is a not for profit organization based out of Denver, USA. It is a comprehensive skill development program to equip participants to offer authentic Financial Plans.
