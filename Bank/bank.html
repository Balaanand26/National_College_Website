B.Com Banking & Finance

B.Com Banking & Finance

National College offers a B.Com in Banking & Finance. This curriculum is designed and structured according to the standards of the BFSI industry, a specialization offered by very few universities. The curriculum places strong emphasis on preparing students to enter the Banking & Financial sector. With the growing BFSI industry, this program offers a bright future and is tailored to meet the evolving needs of the job market. This B.Com program is ideal for those seeking careers in the Banking & Finance sector (BFSI) and is offered as a 3-year degree.

Accreditation

National Institute Of Management & Science in collaboration with Alagappa University ( A State University Accredited with A+ Grade & Category – 1 Graded University by MHRD - UGC) offers Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) Under Graduate Program in Banking & Finance.

Eligibility

Qualification Course
12th or Any Diploma B.Com Banking & Finance

* subject to passing the our Aptitude test followed by Personal Interview.

Salient Features Of The Courses

Industry-Relevant Curriculum :

The curriculum for this banking program has been designed by banking industry experts, which is approved by Alagappa University and has been appreciated by leading banks in India, making it result-oriented.

Experienced Faculties :

All of our trainers are senior banking professionals, and they share their exceptional knowledge, real-life experiences, and case studies to help students grasp the knowledge required to adapt to the banking industry.

Master Classes :

As part of the FinStream curriculum, students have the chance to interact with banking and finance industry experts through selective master classes.

Activities and Workshops :

Activities are undertaken throughout the year to ensure all-round skill development among the students, including presentation techniques, language skills, interview skills, and team building, which makes them industry-ready.

Industry Certification :

We offer training to gain an opportunity for industry-specific certifications like NISM, NCFM, IIBF-CAIIB, CFP, and others.

Career Opportunities

Graduates of the B.Com in Banking & Finance program have a wide range of job opportunities in fields such as banks, mutual fund companies, trading and securities firms, business schools, credit organizations, financial consultancies, economic consulting firms, insurance industry, and other financial institutions.

These opportunities extend beyond the mentioned fields. Upon completion, graduates may either pursue further studies or explore various job opportunities within the BFSI sector.

The BFSI sector offers numerous job prospects with versatility, enabling graduates to seamlessly switch between roles and advance their careers.

Advantages of joining the B.Com Banking & Finance in National College

A B.Com degree awarded by Alagappa University provides specialization in Banking & Finance with a BFSI sector curriculum.

  • International Standards of Banking Trainings.
  • On-the-Job Training in Leading Banks.
  • This program offers Dual Certification, i.e.,a B.Com in Banking & Finance degree from Alagappa University and a Certificate of Accomplishment from National College for a undergraduate Program in Banking & Wealth Management, accredited by the BFSI Sector Skill Council of India.
  • All our trainers are highly experienced professionals from the Banking, Financial Services, and Insurance (BFSI) industry.
