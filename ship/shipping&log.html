BBA Shipping & Logistics

BBA Shipping & Logistics

This curriculum is designed with a conceptual structure aligned to the standards of the Shipping & Logistics industry, offered by very few universities. This curriculum is given a high focus point, allowing students to opt for careers in the Shipping & Logistics sector. In the present and future, there is a bright scope with promising opportunities in the Shipping & Logistics sector, and it is tailored to meet the needs of the job market in the growing global industry.

Accreditation

National Institute of Management & Science, in collaboration with Alagappa University (a State University Accredited with A+ Grade and Category-1 Graded University by MHRD - UGC), offers a Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) undergraduate program in Shipping & Logistics.

Eligibility

Qualification Course
12th or Any Diploma BBA Shipping and Logistics

* subject to passing the our Aptitude test followed by Personal Interview.

Salient Features Of The Courses

Industry-Relevant Curriculum :

The curriculum for this Shipping & Logistics program has been designed by industry experts, approved by Alagappa University, and appreciated by leading Shipping & Logistics companies in India, making it outcome-oriented and industry-relevant.

Experienced Faculties :

All our trainers are senior shipping and logistics professionals who share their exceptional knowledge, real-life experiences, and case studies to help students grasp the knowledge required to adapt to the shipping and logistics industry.

Master Classes :

As part of the FinStream curriculum, students have the opportunity to interact with Shipping & Logistics industry experts through selective master classes.

Activities and Workshops :

Activities are undertaken throughout the year to ensure the all-round development of skills among students, including presentation techniques, language skills, interview skills, and team building. These activities make them industry-ready.

Career Opportunities

Logistics Management graduates can pursue roles like Supply Chain Analyst or Distribution Planner across diverse industries, from manufacturing to e-commerce, ensuring a smooth flow of goods.

Within transportation and logistics companies, graduates can secure positions such as Transportation Manager or Logistics Coordinator, managing the movement of goods and optimizing logistics operations.

In the retail and e-commerce sectors, opportunities abound for graduates to take on roles like Warehouse Operations Manager or Last-Mile Delivery Manager, contributing to the efficient handling and delivery of products in the rapidly growing online marketplace.

Advantage of joining the BBA – Shipping & Logistics in National College

Logistics Management deals with activities related to planning, execution and control of the smooth and consistent flow of goods.

  • Our program ensures that students gain skills directly applicable to the logistics industry, guaranteeing they are well-equipped for roles in procurement, operations, and supply chain management.
  • Through practical training, students develop hands-on experience, significantly increasing their attractiveness to employers across various sectors within the logistics field.
  • Graduates can expect lucrative career paths with annual packages ranging from INR 3 LPA to INR 10 LPA, offering financial stability and growth opportunities.
  • National College provides extensive networking opportunities, connecting students with industry professionals and facilitating valuable connections crucial for future career advancement and success in the logistics sector.
