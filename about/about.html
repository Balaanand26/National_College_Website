About NIMS

National Institute of Management & Science (NIMS) offers World Class Education Programs, Training Sessions and Allied Services in the Area of Aviation, Banking, Fashion Designing, Shipping & Logistics at Our Campuses Strategically located in Madurai, Tirunelveli & Chennai.

About Us

We Committed our Indian National Students to the Job Oriented Program Initiative, NIMS Supports our Students by Providing Top-Notch Training Facilities & Encouraging Career Growth in the Fields of Aviation, Banking, Fashion Designing, Shipping & Logistics.

NIMS has been Globally Integrated with Various Leading Institutes of the world to bring and offer the Best Programmes and courses to our students.

NIMS has made Significant Contributions to the Life and Careers of Thousands of Students and working professionals in the Domain area of Aviation, Banking, Fashion Designing, Shipping & Logistics. Supported by its highly qualified, skilled, and experienced Academicians and Corporate Trainers.

NIMS has witnessed Significant Growth over the years, particularly in the number of Students and trainees, Academic and Training Programs, Campus sites, Faculty & Staff members, Alumni, and various other Dimensions.

Every year, Our College Welcomes Thousands of Students and Trainees from across the World. To Educate, Train and Offer a wide range of our course learning Opportunities Supported by Best-in-Class Research, Expertise, Programs and Facilities.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in providing world-class education and training in Aviation, Banking, Fashion Designing, Logistics, at National and international levels.

Our Mission

To be a leading institution in providing world-class education and training in Aviation, Banking, Fashion Designing, Logistics, at National and international levels.

To Raise the Standard of Performance of the workforce employed in the Aviation sector.

To qualify the Desired Students for successful careers in Software Companies.

To Qualify the Job Seekers in Banks, Train them to apply the concepts and methods of Banking, thereby Augmenting the Existing pool of skilled national human resources.

To increase and enhance the visibility and awareness of the Job Oriented Skill Development Programs to all the Un Employed Graduates.

Maharaja Educational & Charitable Trust

Maharaja Educational & Charitable Trust, founded in memory of Sri. Maharajan in 2017

Maharaja Educational and Charitable Trust's activities are Multi-Faceted in Nature with Education & Promote Social Welfare while placing Importance on National Development.

We Transform Education into Employment Systems to Prepare, Place & Support People into life-changing careers that would otherwise be inaccessible.

We have a Methodology that we can serve People of all ages who are un Employed, Under Employed, or need to learn new skills.
