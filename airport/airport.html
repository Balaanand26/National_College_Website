MBA Airlines & Airport Management

MBA Airlines & Airport Management

The curriculum is advanced and designed as per the standards of the Indian civil aviation industry, offered by very few universities. This curriculum places a high focus on enabling students to enter the aviation sector and is tailored to meet the needs of the job market in the growing aviation industry. This MBA program is designed for those who are seeking jobs in the aviation sector and is a 2-year degree offered under the semester pattern with a specialized management degree.

Accreditation

National Institute Of Management & Science in collaboration with Alagappa University ( A State University Accredited with A+ Grade & Category – 1 Graded University by MHRD - UGC), offers a Master of Business Administration (MBA) Postgraduate Program in Airlines and Airport Management.

Eligibility

Qualification Course
Successfully passed any Bachelor Degree MBA Airlines & Airport Management

* subject to passing the our Aptitude test followed by Personal Interview.

Career Opportunities

This particular MBA course provides multiple opportunities to secure various designations in the industry, such as Air Ticketing, Travel Management, Flight Operations, Customer Service Management, Ramp Service, Passenger Service, Aviation Security Management, Cargo Management, Customer Relationship Management, Revenue Management, Airline Sales & Marketing, Flight/Data Analysis.

Advantages of joining the MBA Airlines and Airport Management in National College

The MBA Degree Awarded by Alagappa University includes a curriculum in Aviation that covers Grooming, Global Communication, Soft Skills & Personality Development.

  • Free In-Flight Training
  • International Standards of Aviation Trainings
  • On-the-Job Training At International Airports
  • All our trainers are well experienced professionals from the aviation industry.
  • The course comes with a Dual Certification, i.e., an MBA in Airlines and Airport Management from Alagappa University and a Certificate of Accomplishment from National College for the Postgraduate Program in Airline Job Roles from the Central Government of India Certification.
